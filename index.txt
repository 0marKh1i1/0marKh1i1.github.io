## Overview

**ASTRO-IMPACT** is a demo website that helps people understand and witness first-hand the portrayal of one of the most serious and undermined very real threats to human civilization: collision with an asteroid. This website achieves that by putting the parameters of a hypothetical asteroid in the hands of the user to test and see the reality of what threats these objects truly pose once they impact Earth.

Impact side effects are made visible and digestible for a wide range of people, including estimated death toll, danger zones of effects like 3rd degree and 2nd degree burns, shockwave propagation, and seismic estimations. With both a "tame" mode and a professional mode, the message can be understood by everyone.

Alongside the actual simulation is a basic guide of mitigative actions that could be executed to minimize the world-altering damage such an event could cause. This project hopes to put more and more people into the right frame of mind relative to this very serious threat to our civilization, and therefore spark the necessary actions to prepare for it.

**Live Project**: [astro-impact.earth](https://astro-impact.earth)

## Key Features

- **Realistic Physics Engine**: Web Worker-based simulations calculate atmospheric entry, crater formation, fireball radius, shockwave propagation, and casualty estimates
- **NASA Integration**: Uses real asteroid data from NASA's Near Earth Object database
- **Two Modes**: 
  - Quick-start Real Mode (5 predefined asteroids √ó 6 countries)
  - Advanced Mode (full parameter control)
- **Educational Focus**: Pre/post-simulation modals teach about planetary defense strategies (NASA DART, gravity tractors, nuclear deflection)
- **3D Visualization**: Real-time animated meteor descent with adjustable speed (0.1x-250x) and intelligent camera systems
- **Interactive 3D Globe**: Built with CesiumJS for realistic Earth visualization and impact location selection

## Technologies Used

- **React JS**: Modern frontend framework for building dynamic user interfaces
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe development for better code quality and maintainability
- **CesiumJS & Resium**: 3D globe visualization and geospatial rendering
- **GLB 3D Models**: Realistic 3D asteroid models
- **NASA APIs**: Real asteroid data integration

## Impact & Purpose

This tool aims to raise awareness about planetary defense, educate the public about asteroid threats, and demonstrate that impacts are preventable with proper preparation. It serves students, educators, and policymakers with scientifically accurate, accessible impact modeling.

By making complex scientific data interactive and visual, ASTRO IMPACT bridges the gap between academic research and public understanding, fostering informed discussions about one of humanity's most significant existential risks.
